About NERC

The Northeast Robotics Colloquium (NERC) brings together researchers, practitioners, and students from across the northeastern United States and Canada to share their latest work in robotics. The event fosters a vibrant exchange of ideas, networking, and collaboration among the region's top robotics institutions.

NERC 2026 will be hosted at Princeton University on October 3, 2026. The program includes keynote talks from distinguished leaders, a Rising Stars oral presentation session, and vibrant poster sessions for students and researchers.

Call for Participation

Rising Stars & Oral Talks

We invite PhD students and postdocs to apply for our Rising Stars session. This year, we are experimenting with featured tutorials by Rising Stars alongside shortened submitted talks, providing a platform for deep technical exchange.

Posters & Demos

Present your latest work during our interactive poster sessions. We heavily lean towards general acceptance of all submissions. This year, we are introducing Oral Awards for Posters, with selected lightning talks before the main session.

Invited Speakers & Panelists

Our program aims to cover a diverse range of robotics specialties, including Humanoid & Robot Learning, HRI, Robot Design, and Controls & Safety. We feature speakers from both academia and industry, with a special focus on recruiting distinguished voices from institutions across the Northeast and beyond.
